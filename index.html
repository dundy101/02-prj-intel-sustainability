1971

Intel introduces groundbreaking microprocessors, powering personal computers and transforming technology for everyone.

The rise of the personal computer brings Intel technology into homes and businesses around the world.

2000s

Intel begins investing in renewable energy, efficient manufacturing, and environmental initiatives to reduce its impact.

The company sets bold goals for climate action, water stewardship, and waste reduction, aiming for a more sustainable future.

Future

Intel aims to advance AI, quantum computing, energy-efficient chips, and integrated photonics to power next-generation computing and connectivity.
